Breakfast Recipes

Start your day right with these delicious breakfast recipes!

Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

A simple and nutritious breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Avocado
  • Whole grain bread
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a skillet.
  2. Toast the bread and spread avocado on top.
  3. Serve the scrambled eggs with the avocado toast.

Oatmeal with Berries

A hearty and healthy breakfast choice.

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk or water
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Cook oats according to package instructions.
  2. Serve hot topped with fresh berries and sweeten with honey or maple syrup.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

A light and refreshing breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Granola
  • Fresh fruit (such as strawberries, bananas, or kiwi)
  • Honey or agave syrup

Instructions:

  1. Layer Greek yogurt, granola, and sliced fruit in a glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey or agave syrup.
  3. Serve chilled.
