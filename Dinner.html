Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish with rich tomato sauce and ground beef.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a skillet with diced onion and minced garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the skillet and simmer until heated through.
  4. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti.
